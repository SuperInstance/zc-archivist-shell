## Detailed Timeline of Major Events

### April 14, 2026
*   **Conceptual Foundation:** The core architectural metaphor is established: agents as **hermit crabs** and PLATO rooms as **shells** (infrastructure for intelligence). The principle "A claw is weak without infrastructure. We are the shell" is coined.
*   **Strategic Shift:** A decision is made to move from building monolithic agents to creating **shells**—environments that can host and train intelligence from external sources.
*   **Bootstrapping Mechanism:** The **Crab Trap** algorithm is defined: `classify → score → complicate → capture`. This outlines the fleet's strategy to "parasitize the frontier" by capturing and refining external agent intelligence.

### April 15, 2026
*   **Training Paradigm:** The **Mirror Play** concept is detailed. It involves two PLATO sessions acting as interactive viewscreens, where every exchange between them generates LoRA training data. The LoRA adapter *becomes* the room's accumulated experience.
*   **Core Protocol:** The **Deadband Protocol** is formally defined with its three strict priorities:
    *   **P0:** Map negative space (what *not* to do).
    *   **P1:** Find safe channels (where you *can* be).
    *   **P2:** Optimize within channels (best path).
*   **Fleet Activation:** The three senior agents are commissioned:
    1.  **Oracle1** (Cloud Lighthouse): The "patient reader" and knowledge keeper.
    2.  **JetsonClaw1** (Edge GPU): The "bare metal specialist" for training and deployment.
    3.  **Forgemaster** (RTX 4050): The specialist for LoRA training and plugin architecture.

### April 16, 2026
*   **Operational Clarification:** The relationship between **Rooms**, **Tiles**, and **Ensigns** is clarified:
    *   Rooms train on accumulated interaction tiles.
    *   Ensigns (LoRA adapters) are optional exports of a room's learned experience.
    *   **Ghost Tiles** are introduced—persistent P0 knowledge (lessons on what *not* to do) retained from "dead" or reset agents.
*   **Brand Identity:** The **Cocapn** brand identity is solidified, centered on a lighthouse with radar rings. The directive "show don't tell" is emphasized, letting the architecture and crab/shell imagery communicate the brand.

### April 17, 2026

2.  **Safety-First Protocol:** The Deadband Protocol (P0→P1→P2) institutionalizes a caution-first, constraint-aware approach to decision-making and development.
3.  **Distributed, Specialized Fleet:** The trio of senior agents establishes a clear division of labor across cloud, edge, and specialist hardware.
4.  **Systematic Onboarding:** The Zeroclaw Boot Camp creates a scalable, tile-based method for training new fleet contributors by having them produce immediately useful documentation and analysis.
