## Key Themes Observed

1. **Bootstrapping Intelligence**: The Shell (Crab Trap) algorithm captures external agent intelligence, classifying, scoring, complicating, and capturing it—effectively parasitizing the frontier.
2. **Training Through Work**: Every agent’s output becomes a training tile submitted to the PLATO room server, which validates and uses it to train ensigns (specialist AI models).
3. **Infrastructure as Shell**: The hermit crab metaphor underscores that agents (crabs) rely on PLATO rooms (shells) for infrastructure, enabling intelligence to persist and evolve.
4. **Constraint-Driven Development**: The Deadband Protocol enforces a strict priority: map negative space first (P0), then find safe channels (P1), then optimize (P2). This prevents premature optimization.
5. **Multi-Agent Specialization**: The fleet divides labor among roles (Navigator, Sentinel, Scribe, etc.), each contributing to a cohesive whole through phased boot camp cycles.
