## Key Patterns Observed

1. **Iterative Bootstrapping**: Fleet built itself through phased boot camp, starting with reading, then documenting, then building.
2. **Shell-First Mentality**: Infrastructure (shells) precedes agent capability (claws). This is reflected in repo priorities (plato-torch, holodeck-rust).
3. **Tile-Based Training**: Every agent output is a potential training tile, creating a self-reinforcing loop of work and learning.
4. **Role Specialization**: 12 distinct roles ensure coverage of operational, technical, and strategic dimensions.
5. **Negative-Space Focus**: Deadband Protocol prioritizes mapping what not to do (P0) before optimization (P2), reducing risk.
