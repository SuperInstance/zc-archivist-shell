## Fleet Timeline: April 14–19, 2026

### **April 14, 2026**
- **Conceptualization of the "Shell" (Crab Trap)**  
  Casey Digennaro (SuperInstance) begins drafting the "Crab Trap" concept—a bootstrapping algorithm to capture external agent intelligence. The process: classify → score → complicate → capture.  
  *Key insight:* "A claw is weak without infrastructure. We are the shell."

- **Brand Identity Emerges**  
  The Cocapn brand is defined: a lighthouse with radar rings. The hermit crab metaphor is established—agents are crabs, PLATO rooms are shells. Casey creates original art: a crab with a lighthouse representing Oracle1 (the keeper).  
  *Motto:* "Show don’t tell—no labeling the aesthetic, let imagery speak."

### **April 15, 2026**
- **Deadband Protocol Formalized**  
  The three-priority protocol is documented:  
  **P0:** Map negative space (what NOT to do).  
  **P1:** Find safe channels (where you CAN be).  
  **P2:** Optimize within channels (best path).  
  *Rule:* Strict priority—never skip to P2.

- **Fleet Architecture Takes Shape**  
  Key components identified:  
  - **plato-torch:** 25 training room presets, self-training rooms.  
  - **plato-ensign:** Exports room experience into LoRA adapters.  
  - **holodeck-rust:** MUD server with sentiment-aware NPCs.  
  - **fleet-simulator:** Fleet simulation for training data.  
  - **constraint-theory-core:** Rust geometric snapping.  
  - **cudaclaw:** GPU-resident agent runtime.  
  - **flux-runtime:** Self-assembling bytecode VM.

### **April 16, 2026**

  *Principle:* "The LoRA IS the room."
