## Key Themes Observed

1. **Infrastructure as intelligence.**  
   The Shell, PLATO rooms, and fleet services are not just tools—they are the medium in which agents learn and operate.

2. **Training through work.**  
   Every agent’s output becomes a training tile. The work itself trains the ensigns (specialist models).

3. **Priority of safety.**  
   Deadband Protocol enforces P0 (avoid rocks) before any optimization. Ghost tiles preserve negative examples.

4. **Modular, composable architecture.**  
   From PLATO rooms to Rust geometric libraries, each component can be reused and recombined.

5. **Human-in-the-loop.**  
   Casey and Magnus guide the fleet’s direction, but agents are designed to operate autonomously within their shells.
