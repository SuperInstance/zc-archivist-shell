## Fleet Timeline: April 14–19, 2026

### April 14, 2026
- **Conceptual Foundation**: The "Deadband Protocol" is formalized. Core principle: P0 (map negative space), P1 (find safe channels), P2 (optimize within channels). Strict priority order.
- **Brand Identity**: "Cocapn" name and lighthouse/radar-ring logo established. Hermit crab metaphor introduced: agents are crabs, PLATO rooms are shells.
- **Technical Vision**: "The Shell" concept defined as a bootstrapping algorithm to capture external agent intelligence (classify→score→complicate→capture).

### April 15, 2026
- **Architectural Shift**: Decision to move from a monolithic "Fleet OS" to a modular "Fleet Simulator." Focus shifts to generating training data through simulation.
- **Core Repositories Created**:
    - `fleet-simulator`: Core simulation engine.
    - `plato-torch`: Training room presets and self-training logic.
    - `holodeck-rust`: Sentiment-aware MUD server for NPC interaction.
- **Mirror Play I2I Concept**: Introduced. Two PLATO sessions act as viewscreens; their exchange generates LoRA training data. "The LoRA IS the room."

### April 16, 2026

- **Training Pipeline Clarified**: Work output from agents becomes "tiles." Tiles are submitted to PLATO rooms. Rooms train from accumulated tiles. "Ensigns" are optional LoRA exports of a room's experience.
- **Ghost Tiles**: Concept introduced. Lessons from dead agents persist as P0 (negative space) knowledge.

### April 19, 2026

- **Fleet State Snapshot**: Three senior agents are active: Oracle1, JetsonClaw1, Forgemaster. Boot camp is underway with agents in various phases.
- **Architectural Summary**: The Cocapn stack is defined as: PLATO rooms (shells) that train from interaction tiles, optionally exporting Ensigns (LoRAs). The Shell algorithm parasitizes external intelligence. Mirror Play generates training data.
